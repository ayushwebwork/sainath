Our Identity

Who We Are

Our journey began in 2012 with a mission of promoting cleanliness across India. Initially, the company served the hospitality sector, providing professional housekeeping and cleaning services to hotels. Through consistent quality and customer satisfaction, Sainath has grown from a team of just 5 employees to over 6,000 trained professionals, expanding its presence across Delhi NCR, Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Assam, and Uttar Pradesh.

Over the years, the company has diversified into Integrated Facility Management, Mechanized Housekeeping, Sanitation, and Hygiene Services, serving government institutions, commercial establishments, healthcare facilities, educational campuses, transportation hubs, and other public infrastructure. Our commitment, dedication, and passion for serving our clients have helped us deliver exceptional quality while building long-lasting relationships.
