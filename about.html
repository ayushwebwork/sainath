Our Identity

Who We Are

Our journey began in 2012 with a mission of maintaining cleanliness across India. Initially the company served only hotel clients. Sainath is one of the best quality-service providers in the sector. The company has grown from 5 employees in Nagpur (Dhantoli Prashant) to over 5,000 professionals today, acquiring the best talent in Delhi (NCR), Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Assam, and UP.

The company has now graduated into the Facility Management and Sanitation & Hygiene space. Our commitment, dedication, and passion to serve our customers have led us to grow ever further—and we look forward to growing our ever-lasting relationship with them through diligent due diligence.
